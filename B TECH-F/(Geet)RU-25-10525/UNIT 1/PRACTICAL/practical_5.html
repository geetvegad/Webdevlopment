HTML Basics

HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the foundation of all web pages. Here’s a simple example of HTML structure:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
  <title>My First Page</title>
</head>
<body>
  <h1>Hello, CodeVerse!</h1>
  <p>Welcome to the world of HTML.</p>
</body>
</html>
